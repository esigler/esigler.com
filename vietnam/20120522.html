Reactions when people learned of my travel plans included:

  • "Haven't the Vietnamese people suffered enough?"

Another reason I'm going is the phrase "to be old and wise you must first be young and stupid". Not the "young and stupid" part - no, it's that I'm slowly, painfully, inch by inch becoming aware of the flaws and faults I have, while I walk along the path to "old". I'm learning when it's time to take a vacation, although I'm still working on figuring this out before it's time. So, you could say I'm going halfway around the world so my friends/family/coworkers don't kill me.
